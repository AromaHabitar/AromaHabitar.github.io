Aroma Habitar

Perfume Artisan Brand · Davis, California

Aroma Habitar is a perfume artisan brand rooted in the heart of Davis, California, where a rich variety of plants flourish in every season. Inspired by this lush and ever-changing landscape, we believe that good scents are the habitat of valuable memories.

Each bottle we create captures the living essence of California’s year-round botanical abundance, from the uplifting bloom of spring to the cozy depth of fall. Our fragrances aim to preserve these cherished moments and share the joy they bring with others.

To reach more scent lovers and share our creations globally, we are preparing to launch on Etsy. As a thoughtful first step, we are developing a market research tool that leverages Etsy’s public product listing data.

This project will help us fine-tune our product designs, marketing strategies, and campaign planning on Etsy based on real-world insights into:

  • Etsy's customer preferences in fragrance categories
  • Popular product positioning and scent profiles
  • Effective title wording and tags
  • Competitor pricing benchmarks

We are deeply committed to creating products that not only smell beautiful but also connect meaningfully with people — just like the memories they evoke.

This website is part of an independent project using the Etsy API to study product trends, pricing, and keyword data in the fragrance market. We are committed to using only publicly available product listings to better understand the marketplace and refine our marketing strategy, with the goal of attracting more traffic and visibility for our upcoming Etsy shop.
